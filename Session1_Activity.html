Ichigo Kurosaki is a Shinigami.

His goal is simple. To protect his loved ones. His name literally means he who protects.

Shinigamis are guardians of the souls who are going through the circle of transmigration.

Ichigo Kurosaki is the main character of hit manga, Bleach by Tite Kubo

About

Ichigo Kurosaki is a Human wwho is also a Substitute Shinigami. Ichigo is the son of Isshin and Masaki Kurosaki, and older brother of Karin and Yuzu. He is also married to Orihime Inoue and has a son named Kazui Kurosaki.
